Help & Support

Welcome to PrimeKart Support. Here you’ll find answers to common questions, our contact details, and a way to reach us directly for any assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Go to the “My Orders” section in your account dashboard to track your order in real-time.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Credit/Debit cards, Mobile Banking (Bkash, Nagad, Rocket), and Cash on Delivery.

How do I return or replace a product?

You can request a return or replacement within 7 days of delivery through the “Returns” page.
