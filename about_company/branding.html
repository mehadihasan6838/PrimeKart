Brand Guidelines

The PrimeKart brand represents trust, innovation, and simplicity. Always use the official logo, colors, and typography to maintain a consistent and professional brand image.

Avoid altering the logo, stretching it, or using unofficial colors. Ensure adequate contrast and clarity when displaying brand elements in digital or print media.

For any questions regarding brand usage, contact our branding team at branding@primekart.com.
